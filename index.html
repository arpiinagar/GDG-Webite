ABOUT
DevFests are large, community-run developer conferences organized around the globe with a focus on community building and learning about various Google technologies. Women Techmakers and Developer Student Clubs Manipal bring to you DevFest 2018.

Join us on the 23rd of September for one day of talks and workshops on latest Google technologies ranging from Actions on Google to Material Design and Flutter.

This year, we have two concurrent tracks and the attendees may choose to attend one of these based on their interests.

The WTM track will kickoff with an introduction to Material Design, UI/UX and then will move on to mobile app development with Flutter.

The Developer Students' Club will walk the participants through developing Actions For Google Assistant alongside covering Firebase
