About

DevFest is one of a kind Hackathon that goes forth with the aim of encouraging healthy competition in order to incubate futuristic ideas. DevFest focuses on providing student and upcoming entrepreneurs with a platform to develop, compete, network and present their ideas to solve real-world problems. We have been succesfully conducting DevFest for 4 years now. DevFest'19 will be the fifth edition and we hope to bring the innovative minds from all over the coutry under one roof.
